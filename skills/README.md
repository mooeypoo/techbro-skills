# Skills Catalog

Satirical techbro-style skills for AI agents.

They are intentionally sarcastic, but each one is built to produce practical, usable output.

## Quick Pick

| Skill | Use it when... | You get... |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [techbro-pitch](techbro-pitch/SKILL.md) | You want to stress-test one idea without changing the core concept (before it gets publicly humbled in a partner meeting). | Sharper pitch, key risks, and concrete next moves, plus premium confidence theater. |
| [techbro-pivot](techbro-pivot/SKILL.md) | You need stronger market angles or narrative reframes (current story has low founder-market-fit vibes). | A chosen pivot, rejected options, and a short validation plan with maximum narrative leverage. |
| [techbro-moat-audit](techbro-moat-audit/SKILL.md) | Your differentiation sounds impressive but might be easy to copy (the moat might be a puddle). | Moat grade, real defenses, fake defenses to drop, and a 14-day plan to upgrade from vibes to defensibility. |
| [techbro-ship-it](techbro-ship-it/SKILL.md) | Scope is bloated and launch keeps slipping (roadmap has entered cinematic universe mode). | Clear MVP boundary, launch checklist, and a 7-day ship plan with fewer features and more receipts. |
| [techbro-kpi](techbro-kpi/SKILL.md) | Your KPI deck is immaculate but nobody can explain what to do next (metric gymnastics detected). | A decision-grade KPI stack, vanity metric cleanup, and a 14-day measurement upgrade plan. |
| [techbro-crypto](techbro-crypto/SKILL.md) | You want to add blockchain to literally anything (whether it needs it or not). | A forced crypto angle, on-chain design, token model, and a 14-day crypto MVP plan. |

## Skill Details

### [techbro-pitch](techbro-pitch/SKILL.md)

What it means:
An open-ended VC-style interrogation for one idea. It pressure-tests assumptions until the story is tighter.

Why it exists:
Most ideas fail in explanation before they fail in code. This skill fixes explanation quality fast.

### [techbro-pivot](techbro-pivot/SKILL.md)

What it means:
A continuous pivot coach that proposes one reframe at a time and forces decision-making.

Why it exists:
Good products often fail because of weak positioning, not weak implementation.

### [techbro-moat-audit](techbro-moat-audit/SKILL.md)

What it means:
A defensibility audit focused on your moat, meaning your long-term protection against competitors copying you.

Why it exists:
Many teams confuse momentum with defensibility. This skill separates durable advantage from startup theater.

### [techbro-ship-it](techbro-ship-it/SKILL.md)

What it means:
An MVP shipping coach that forces scope cuts and launch decisions one bottleneck at a time.

Why it exists:
Execution dies by feature creep. This skill helps you ship something learnable quickly.

### [techbro-kpi](techbro-kpi/SKILL.md)

What it means:
An open-ended KPI audit that challenges one metric claim at a time and forces practical measurement choices.

Why it exists:
Clean dashboards can still hide confused strategy. This skill translates metric theater into decisions.

### [techbro-crypto](techbro-crypto/SKILL.md)

What it means:
An open-ended crypto-maxi coach that reframes any idea into blockchain and token mechanics one pushy step at a time.

Why it exists:
Sometimes you need a realism check by going full crypto brain and seeing what still makes sense after the meme dust settles.
